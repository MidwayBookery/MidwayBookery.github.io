About Us

Dedicated to the creation, consumption, admiration, and discussion of literature. Striving to be the perfect home for all Bibliophiles, Bibliophages, Logophiles, and anyone who loves the written format, we provide a hub for tons of book-related content and conversations!

Our club's name and branding stem from an online community of the same name. It was founded by Zachary Montgomery

Get Involved

We meet weekly in the library, from 4:15 to 4:45 to discuss what we've read that week. Our tastes are widely varied and we choose a different book for every month, along with plenty of fun events all throughout the month. Come to the library and join us!

Want a sneak peak into what's going on right now? Take a look at our Instagram for the latest from us.
